My Mom

Basima R.

My Mom grew up in New York for her entire life. She has a younger brother and two older half siblings. She swam in high school and during her time as a student at CCNY where she met Julian Abreu, my dad. They eventually got married and later had me and my siblings. She worked in marketing at an architecture firm until she stopped due to medical reasons and became a stay at home mom. I have seen her in pain, in true happiness, in the funniest moods, and I can't help but look up to her. My mom is my hero.

Reasons Why My Mom Is My Hero
  • She takes care of me
  • She does everything she can to make me and my siblings happy
  • She makes great food
  • She is funny and makes me smile when I am feeling upset
  • She supports me
  • She endured the unimaginable pain of childbirth three times
  • She is not afraid to embarass herself to teach me lessons
  • She makes time for volunteering and is part of the PTO/PTA for three different schools
  • She always gives her best
  • She knows English, some Spanish, and is teaching herself Arabic
  • She is patient and accepting
  • She puts everyones needs before her own
  • She is confident but modest
  • She owns her opinions and taught me to do the same
  • She puts everything aside, headache or sadness, to make us happy
